MY SKILLS

As a full-time mother these days, I don't always have the time I used to devote to Graphic Design. I would say that as a Designer, I specialize in creating logos and illustrations. I am taking Colt Steele's Web Developer Bootcamp on Udemy and am on my way to learn Javascript. However, I took a brief break to create this responsive portfolio page, as a way to review what I've learned of Bootstrap/CSS concepts.
