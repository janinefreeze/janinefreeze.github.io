MY SKILLS

As a full-time mother these days, I don't always have the time I used to devote to Graphic Design. I would say that as a Designer, I specialize in creating logos and illustrations. I've also found a renewed passion for coding. I learned the very basics of HTML and CSS as a college student, but am currently learning full-stack development. I am taking Colt Steele's Web Developer Bootcamp on Udemy and am on my way to learn Javascript.
